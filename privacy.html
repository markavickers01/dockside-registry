DockSide Fleet — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

DockSide Fleet ("the App") is operated by Mark Vickers. This privacy policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information from users:

  • Email address — used for authentication and identifying your account within your franchise company's system.
  • PIN — used for login. Your PIN is transmitted securely and verified against your company's backend server. It is not stored on the device in plain text.
  • Push notification token — a device-specific identifier generated by your device's operating system, used to deliver push notifications about inspections, service requests, and other work-related alerts.
  • Inspection and service request data — information you enter while performing inspections, creating issues, incidents, and service requests as part of your job duties.
  • Photos — images you capture within the App as part of inspections and service requests. Photos are uploaded directly to your company's Salesforce system.

2. How We Use Your Information

All information collected is used solely for the purpose of fleet management operations on behalf of the Freedom Boat Club franchise company you work for. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and provide access to your company's fleet management data.
  • To deliver push notifications relevant to your work assignments.
  • To record inspection results, service requests, and related operational data.
  • To store and display photos associated with inspection and service request records.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in the following locations:

  • Salesforce — your company's Salesforce organization stores all inspection data, service requests, photos, notification records, and staff records. Each franchise company maintains its own separate Salesforce environment. Data is fully isolated between companies.
  • Device storage — authentication tokens are stored in encrypted on-device storage (SecureStore). Reference data caches are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage to improve performance. This data is cleared when you sign out.
  • Backend servers — the App communicates with backend servers hosted on Render. These servers act as intermediaries between the App and Salesforce. Push notification tokens are held temporarily in server memory and are also persisted in Salesforce for reliability.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is accessible only to:

  • The franchise company you are employed by, through their Salesforce system.
  • The App's backend infrastructure, solely for the purpose of relaying data between your device and Salesforce.

The App does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Retention

Data retention is managed by your franchise company through their Salesforce organization. The App operator does not independently retain your personal data beyond what is necessary for the App to function. When you sign out of a company in the App, locally cached data for that company is cleared from your device and your push notification token is deregistered from the company's backend.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted device storage.
  • All communication between the App and backend servers uses HTTPS.
  • Each franchise company has its own isolated backend and Salesforce environment with a unique security key.
  • Record locking prevents conflicting edits between users.
  • Session timeouts automatically log out inactive users, with stricter timeouts for shared devices.

7. Your Choices

You can sign out of any company at any time through the App's Profile screen. Signing out clears your locally stored data and deregisters your device from receiving push notifications for that company. Contact your franchise company's administrator to request modification or deletion of your data in Salesforce.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is a workplace tool intended for use by employees of Freedom Boat Club franchise companies. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
